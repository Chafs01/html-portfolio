The Best Movies According To Jordan

My Top 5 Movies

No Country For Old Men

This is my favorite movie of all time. Javier Bardem is amazing at depicting a psycopath.

Shutter Island

An amazing thriller that has you thinking after the movie is done.

The Green Mile

An all-time classic that tells a gutwrenching story.

Parasite

Perhaps the best foreign film of all time. A thrilling and unique story.

Inception

A Christopher Nolan masterpiece. Perhaps the best science fiction movie of all time.
